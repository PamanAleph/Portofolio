## Overview

Laduny Computer Store is the first computer shop in **Demak City**, established in the 90s. As the first computer shop, Laduny Computer wants to expand its business to be bigger and more modern by utilizing technology to reach more consumers. To achieve this, the team developed a **web-based information management system** that allows customers to **request services, track repairs, explore product catalogs, and interact with technicians** online.

## Key Features

- **Request Services & Consultation**: Customers can submit service requests and chat with technicians online, reducing unnecessary travel.
- **Track Service**: Customers can track the repair status of their devices in real-time, eliminating the need to repeatedly contact customer support.
- **Product Catalog**: An online catalog displaying Laduny Computer Store's products, complete with specifications, stock availability, and direct purchase links.
- **Admin Dashboard**: A centralized system for monitoring service requests, product updates, and customer interactions using **graph-based analytics**.
- **User Management**: Secure authentication system allowing customers to create accounts and manage their service history.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend**: Built with **Next.js and TypeScript** for a responsive and modern UI.
- **Backend**: Developed using **Golang**, ensuring high performance and scalability.
- **Database**: **MySQL** for structured data storage, ensuring efficient retrieval of customer and product information.
- **Authentication**: Implemented secure login and registration for customer accounts.
- **Performance Optimization**:
  - **Optimized queries** to ensure minimal database load.
  - **Lazy loading** for product images and components.

## Challenges and Learnings

1. **Enhancing User Experience**
   - Designed an intuitive **service request process** that allows real-time consultations with technicians.
   - Improved navigation and UI components to streamline service tracking and product browsing.

2. **Scalability & Performance**
   - Optimized MySQL queries to handle concurrent requests efficiently.

3. **Security & Authentication**
   - Secure login system with role-based access control for **customers and admins**.
   - Ensured data privacy using **encrypted connections** and best security practices.

## Outcome

The Laduny Computer Store platform has successfully streamlined customer interactions by enabling **online service requests, real-time tracking, and product exploration**. Future improvements will focus on enhancing customer engagement and expanding the platform's features, such as integrating a **loyalty program and AI-driven recommendations** for products and services.
